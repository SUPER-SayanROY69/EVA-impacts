Top Attractions

Home

Srinagar

Famous for its mesmerizing Dal Lake, houseboats, and Mughal gardens, Srinagar offers a picturesque escape in the Himalayas.

Srisailam

A sacred Hindu pilgrimage site known for the Mallikarjuna Jyotirlinga temple and the serene Srisailam Dam.

Darjeeling

Renowned for its tea gardens, the Toy Train, and breathtaking views of Kanchenjunga, Darjeeling is a paradise for nature lovers.

Nepal

Home to Mount Everest and a rich cultural heritage, Nepal is a haven for trekkers and spiritual seekers.

Vishakapatnam

A coastal gem known for its beautiful beaches, the INS Kursura Submarine Museum, and the scenic Araku Valley.

Udaipur

The "City of Lakes," Udaipur is famed for its stunning palaces, serene lakes, and vibrant culture.
